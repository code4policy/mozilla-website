Spicy Double Chocolate Cookie

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet win tinfoil.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, cocoa, chilli powder, and cinnamon.
  3. With a mixer, cream butter and sugars together in a separate bowl until smooth. Add in egg and vanilla and mix until combined.
  4. Gradually add flour mixture and beat until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand. Scoop the dough into rounded tablespoons and place on prepared baking sheet, about 2 inches apart. Bake cookies for 8-10 minutes, or until cookies are set, but still soft in the center. Don't overbake. Remove from oven and let sit on baking sheet for 3 minutes. Move to a cooling rack and cool completely.
